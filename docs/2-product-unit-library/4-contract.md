## Regulatory

### Regulatory/Contractual Product Units

Compliance, registration, and legal documentation services.

#### Registration Services

**Vehicle Registration**
- Initial registration processing
- Registration renewal
- Transfer of ownership documentation

**Compliance Certification**
- Safety inspections
- Emissions certification
- Regulatory compliance verification

#### Legal Documentation

**Contract Preparation**
- Purchase agreements
- Service contracts
- Lease documentation

**Insurance Coordination**
- Insurance registration assistance
- Claims support documentation
- Policy coordination

### Regulatory Product Characteristics

**Common Attributes:**
- Jurisdiction-specific requirements
- Renewal cycles
- Documentation standards
- Compliance deadlines

**Obligations:**
- Accurate documentation
- Timely submission
- Regulatory liaison
- Customer notification of requirements

**Context Requirements:**
- Valid customer identification
- Proof of ownership or authorization
- Jurisdiction verification
- Fee payment
