## Taxonomy

### Product-Unit Categories

Product-Units are classified into the following categories:

#### 1. Physical Product Units

* Battery packs
* Vehicles (LEV E3-Pro, S3, etc.)
* Chargers
* Accessories
* Installation kits

#### 2. Service Product Units

* Swap Subscription
* Warranty extension
* Maintenance plans
* Support services

#### 3. Deposit-Based Entitlement Units

* Asset pool access deposits
* Refundable security deposits
* Convertible value deposits

#### 4. Digital/Operational Product Units

* App onboarding
* IoT tracking features
* CARM capabilities
* Platform access

#### 5. Regulatory/Contractual Product Units

* Registration services
* Compliance add-ons
* Legal documentation

#### 6. Composite Product Units

Meaningful multi-component product-units that combine elements from multiple categories.
