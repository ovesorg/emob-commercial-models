## Composite

### Composite Product Units

Meaningful multi-component product-units that combine elements from multiple categories.

#### Common Composite Units

**Starter Kit**
- Physical: Vehicle + Charger + Accessories
- Service: Initial setup and training
- Digital: App onboarding
- Total package for new customer activation

**Delivery Driver Pack**
- Physical: Vehicle (commercial grade)
- Service: Extended warranty + priority support
- Deposit: Battery pool access deposit
- Digital: Fleet management access

**Multi-Battery Efficiency Bundle**
- Physical: 2x Battery packs
- Service: Unlimited swap subscription
- Deposit: Enhanced deposit for multiple assets
- Digital: Advanced analytics access

### Composite Unit Characteristics

**Design Principles:**
- Address specific customer use cases
- Provide economic incentive vs. individual components
- Simplify sales and onboarding
- Reduce agent decision complexity

**Risks to Manage:**
- Hidden obligations across components
- Cost/value misalignment
- System (Odoo/App) bundling constraints
- Customer misunderstanding of what's included
- Over-complexity for agents

**Value Proposition Requirements:**
- Clear pricing advantage vs. individual purchase
- Coherent customer narrative
- Simplified fulfillment workflow
- Aligned obligations across all components

### Composite Unit Template

```markdown
# Composite Product-Unit: [Name]

## Components
- [Physical components]
- [Service components]
- [Digital components]
- [Deposit components]
- [Regulatory components]

## Bundle Value Proposition
- Customer benefit vs. individual purchase
- Use case alignment
- Economic incentive

## Combined Obligations
- Aggregated service levels
- Warranty coverage across components
- Support requirements

## Bundle Pricing
- Individual component prices
- Bundle discount
- Total bundle price

## Fulfillment Dependencies
- Sequence of delivery/activation
- Integration requirements
- Customer acceptance criteria
```
