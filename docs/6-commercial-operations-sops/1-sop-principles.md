## SoP Principles

!!! info "Asset-Service Contract Operations"
    Service fulfillment and aftersales operations involving physical assets (motorbikes, batteries) bound to service contracts (warranties, swap privileges) follow the **[Assets & Serial Numbers](https://docs.omnivoltaic.com/odoo-migration/assets-serial-numbers/)** architecture. Key operational principles:
    
    - Every service contract binds to a specific serial number (1-to-1)
    - Odoo maintains commercial contract validity
    - ABS enforces operational entitlements
    - Amendments/replacements originate in Odoo, propagate to ABS
